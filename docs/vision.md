# Vision: FoodProof DK

## Goal
Empower Danish consumers to make informed, healthy choices by instantly cross-referencing food labels with official guidelines from **Sundhedsstyrelsen** (The Danish Health Authority).

## Core Principles
- **Accuracy**: Every claim is backed by official Sundhedsstyrelsen data.
- **Simplicity**: Scan a label, get a clear "Proof" or "Warning" based on nutritional thresholds.
- **Localization**: Tailored specifically for the Danish market, language, and dietary recommendations.

## Target Experience
A user scans a product in a Danish supermarket. The app identifies the product, analyzes the ingredients/nutrition, and displays how it aligns with the official "De Officielle Kostråd" (The Official Dietary Guidelines).

## Next Milestones
1. **Scraper/API Integration**: Map out how to programmatically access Sundhedsstyrelsen nutritional guidelines.
2. **Label Recognition**: Implement OCR/Vision logic to parse Danish food labels.
3. **Scoring Engine**: Develop the logic that translates raw nutrition data into a "Sundhedsstyrelsen-approved" status.
